Amusing Musings

by Gary H. Grumbley II

First Post!

September 12, 2014

This is my first blog post on my new homepage. The purpose of this blog is to give me a place to address any subject that I find interesting. The title "Amusing Musings" was taken from one my favorite comic series called Too Much Coffee Man. Here are some of the areas I will be writing about:

  • Software Development, all issues related to developing great modern applications. I have been interested in computers my whole life but only started taking programming seriously in the last few years. So it seems like I am always learning something new. I will try to post insights in my journey from novice to journeyman.
  • Intellectual Property Law, I went to law school to study legal issues related to the technology industry. While securities and employment law are important to start ups I found myself drawn to IP licensing. It is such an interesting area and I hope to chronicle many evolving IP issues here.
  • Books, specifically Science Fiction, Fantasy, non-fiction and graphic novels.
  • Technology, I am interested in all areas of technology from wearables to cleantech to biotech. We live in exciting times and my childlike sense of wonder will be on full display.

I hope this served as a small primer for the type of material that excites me and that I will be discussing here.
